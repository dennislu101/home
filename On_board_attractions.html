On Board Attractions

Things to do on board.

SPAS

The Norwegian Breakaway has a very modern, beautiful Day Spa, Medi Spa,
Salon and Fitness Center operated by Mandara Spa – spanning 23,000 square feet
and encompassing two decks. There are over 50 spa treatments including Elemis
facials, Bamboo or hot-stone massages, and even an oxygen treatment from bliss®.
Norwegian Breakaway also has acupuncture, teeth whitening
and cosmetic medical treatments, performed by a licensed medi-spa doctor,
such as Botox® Cosmetic, Dysport(TM), Restylane® and Perlane®.
In the Breakaway’s Thermal Suite, you can melt away your stress in heated loungers,
hot tubs, the vitality pool, steam rooms and a dry sauna.
Within the Thermal Suite is one of the most anticipated additions to Norwegian’s
spa lineup – the Salt Room. Very trendy, salt therapy has been proven to alleviate respiratory
and skin ailments, strengthen the immune system, promote better sleep and have a long-term
positive impact on overall lung function.

Sports Court and others

The Norwegian Breakaway has a full and luxurious sports court
including basketball soccer and tennis facilities!! The court
Competitions are hosted every day. The top deck is also home
to something called a skywalk which is basically like a ropes
course. What's impressive is that there is a plank that spans
eight feet over the side of the ship so it feels like you are
about to fall! There is also a rock climbing facility and a mini
golf course.

Water fun!

The Breakaway has two main pools and two hot tubs. They are comfortable
in size and the hot tubs according to a website is around 39.4 degrees
celsius which is more than comfortable. The Breakaway also has five water
slides making it the largest water park of the Norwegian Cruise Line.
The most notable one is the twin free-fall one where it is essentially
a waterslide that vertically drops you from a two story height and twist
and tangle through the tubes. It is also a double, meaning that you can
race your friends.

Entertainment

There are a wide variety of shows on board including Burn the Floor and
The Second City. Also the shows are average, they are worth going for because
they are unique. I mean who watches shows in the middle of the ocean? What I do
recommend is the casino for adults as there are a wide variety of games that
that you could waste your money on. A wide variety of bars also means that you
could waste some more money on beers and cocktails to get drunk on. And the perfect
place to do that, would be of course..at the many bars on the ship.
