Norwegian Breakaway

Introduction.

Cruise line has to offer. At 1068.3 feet in length, a guest capacity of up to 3963 people and refurbished in 2018, the Breakaway is
sure to make your dream vacation a reality. Sit back and relax and enjoy up to 14 days of tropical sunshine.
